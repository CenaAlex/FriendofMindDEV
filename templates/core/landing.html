Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our mental health support platform

Is my information secure and private?

Yes, absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data, and we never share your personal information without your explicit consent. Your privacy is our top priority.

Are the assessments scientifically validated?

Yes, all our assessments (PHQ-9, GAD-7, and PSS) are scientifically validated tools used by mental health organizations worldwide. They provide reliable insights into your mental health status.

Can I replace therapy with this platform?

No, FriendofMind is designed to complement, not replace, professional therapy. We provide screening tools and resources, but for ongoing treatment, we recommend working with qualified mental health organizations.

How much does it cost to use FriendofMind?

Basic assessments and resources are completely free. We believe mental health support should be accessible to everyone. Some premium features may be available in the future.

What should I do if I'm in crisis?

If you're having thoughts of self-harm or are in immediate danger, please call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room. You can also call the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 988 for 24/7 support.
